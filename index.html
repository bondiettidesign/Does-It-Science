Beneath the Surface

Brian Bondietti · BI-141 Marine Habitats · 2025

Before you is my BI-141 Marine Habitats finals project. During my creative writing fiction classes here at PCC, I started working on a science fiction novel about sentient octopi trying to save humanity from themselves. I decided to use my finals project in this class to see how closely my story adheres to actual science as learned in our class together.

I have provided four chapters meant to be the beginning of the novel. You can read them if you want to see what the story is about.

If you would prefer to just do notes on my project, scroll to the "Does It Science" section and click on the cards that say "Research Notes."

Finally, I'm providing a series of vignettes for those who actually enjoyed the story. In the page called "Apocrypha," you can see how I'm working through some ideas that will help me flesh out the story and the personalities of the characters.

Thanks for checking it out.
Brian
